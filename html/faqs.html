FAQs :)
Is MAYES.PH a registered business entity?
Yes, MAYES.PH is a 100% Filipino-owned corporation and was SEC registered back in October 2021.
Is MAYES.PH an HMO or Insurance company?
No. MAYES.PH is a benefits solutions company that is accredited by established HMO and Insurance companies to sell their products / services on their behalf. MAYES.PH provides options for MSME's to select their preferred HMO and / or Group Life Insurance that will fit their requirement.
Is the HRIS technology being offered by MAYES.PH really free?
The cloud based HRIS (CHRIS-Pay) is indeed free! The system is bundled with the HMO availed by the client.
What is the Payroll Outsourcing solution that MAYES.PH is offering?
MAYES.PH will process your Payroll at a fraction of the cost that leading payroll outsourcing companies are offering, we'll give you this while you make your employees smile! We can even give it for free as long as the HMO you'll be availing meets the required MBL (Maximum Benefit Limit).
Are you offering other benefits or rewards solution?
Yes. MAYES.PH can help you develop benefits and rewards program that will fit your budget while achieving the objectives of the program. Furthermore, we will soon launch a market place for flexible benefits that your employees can avail.
What is the employee loan solution that you are offering?
We recognize the fact that a lot of employees find it difficult to access interest friendly financing companies, where they can avail of loans easily especially during emergencies. We partnered with loan providers that offer low interest rates and can be accessed immediately. This offering can be availed along with the HMO and Payroll outsourcing as we'll help in the processing and facilitation of the employees' loans.
Can we opt to avail just your HRIS and Payroll Outsourcing solutions?
This option can be explored. However, the price points will vary based on the number of your employees, and will be higher than the HMO bundled offering. Kindly contact us to discuss this further.
